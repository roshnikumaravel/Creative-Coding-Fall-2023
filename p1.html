finding janneane's bracelet

What does Janneane's bracelet look like?

"The bracelet has a delicate chain of gold links, slightly tarnished. There are maybe eight tiny flowers, spaced out evenly between the links. The flowers are diamond-shaped, composed of 4 gems that are very light blue, almost white."

Reflections

Janneane's descriptive language, using words like "delicate" and "tarnished" painted a detailed picture of what her lost bracelet looked like. Since the bracelet is composed of many tiny pieces repeated over and over again, I used a loop to "construct" the bracelet based on her description.
